Member Groups

Rather than sort our members into dull categories based on species or faction, we decided to go the fun route with aesthetic-based groups! These are purely subjective, and based on some of the key plants native to Cape Breton Island. Pick whichever group you feel suits your character the best - it doesn't have to be perfect!

Amaranth

combat boots with a bit of a heel / knowing when to blend in and when to stand out / heavy on the eyeliner / getting back up when you’re knocked down / stuck between two worlds / dark humor to cover up the pain

Birch

fluid and resilient / backpacking across europe / the “mom friend” / getting up at a reasonable hour / finding the silver linings / a forest in wintertime / bringing coffee for everybody / the first to arrive and the last to leave

Blueberry

sweet as punch / seeing the best in everyone / knowing everybody’s love languages / making flower crowns out of whatever you can reach / laughing until you can’t breathe / eating dessert before dinner

Dandelion

carefree and creative / the loudest laugh in the room / clothes that don’t quite match / showing up fifteen minutes late with starbucks / all-nighters fueled by redbull / knows how to roll with the punches

Hemlock

melancholy / the patter of rain against a window / soft piano music / bruises you don’t remember getting / being talked over in a room full of people / feeling the wind on your face / giving your last piece of food to someone that needs it more

Juniper

volatile and unpredictable / matching tattoos with people you don’t talk to anymore / hurting others before they can hurt you / feeling the bass reverberate through your chest / “what are you looking at?” / mosh pits / staying high so you don't crash

Lupin

stronger than you look / often underestimated / keeping the peace / crying out of frustration / prefers libraries to bookstores / asking the hard questions / embracing the past instead of moving on / fear of disappointing others

Nettle

picking fights just to feel alive / the smell of alcohol fresh on your breath / assuming the worst and pretending you accept it / wicked smiles that don’t reach your eyes / a glare that could cut through glass / lights reflected in a rain puddle

Spruce

stoic and solid / more left-brained than right / resting bitch face / wise beyond your years / wearing flannel year-round / the “hard sciences” / change is the worst constant / dramatic eye-rolls and dry laughter

Sundew

as sweet as they are deadly / femme fatales / sneaking out before sunrise / the taste of bitterness on your lips / breaking hearts like they broke yours / keeping to the shadows / lipstick on the rim of a glass
